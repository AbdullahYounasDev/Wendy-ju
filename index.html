Resume

Hey there, it's Wendy. I’m a product designer who is currently pursuing a Master's degree in Human-Computer Interaction at Carnegie Mellon University.

The interest lies in crafting valuable experiences that bridge humanized technology and social wellness centered around meeting people’s needs. Having lived in China, UK, and US in the past 20 years, I’ve developed a unique strength in empathizing and forming emotional connections with people from diverse backgrounds. Through listening to their stories, I've gained a deeper understanding and empathy for those around me. As a designer, my passion goes beyond solving user problems. I want to use my abilities to tell their stories , to give a voice to those who might not have one otherwise. I want to leverage my skills in design thinking and quality craftsmanship to create solutions that not only solve problems, but empower the rich diversity of the human experience.

Previously, I received my Bachelor’s degree in Communications Design from Pratt Institute and a minor in sustainability. I was also a designer at Agenda NYC and have worked on enterprise-scale digital products for clients across a wide range of sectors. Combining visual storytelling with a user-centered approach in tackling complex problem spaces is my special sauce.
